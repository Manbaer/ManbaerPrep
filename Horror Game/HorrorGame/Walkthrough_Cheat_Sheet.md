# Walkthrough Cheat Sheet

Full spoilers. How to beat every day, every dream, and reach all four endings.

Controls: WASD move, mouse look, E interact, Esc pause. The task list in the top-left shows what today needs. The bed only works once all of the day's tasks are done.

## Day 1 - The Routine

Tasks (any order):

1. Check the TV (living room, south wall).
2. Check the answering machine (living room, west wall).
3. Turn off the kitchen light (switch on the kitchen's north wall).

Then sleep in the bed (bedroom, north wall).

### Dream 1: The Endless Hallway

The hallway has five sections. Each section has five props (painting, lamp, telephone, clock, mirror), but only ONE per section is wrong - marked with a red floating sign. Interact with the wrong one in each section, in ANY order:

- Section 1: the upside-down painting
- Section 2: the flickering lamp
- Section 3: the ringing telephone
- Section 4: the backward clock
- Section 5: the mirror that does not show you

Touching any normal prop resets everything and sends you back to the start. Each fix slides the bedroom door closer. When all five are fixed, open the BEDROOM DOOR at the end.

## Day 2 - Something Is Different

Tasks: listen to the new answering machine message, look at the changed bedroom photo, check the covered bathroom mirror, look in the fridge. Then sleep.

Memorize what you saw - the next dream tests it.

### Dream 2: The Wheat Field

Read the FUSE BOX near the start: "THE HOUSE REMEMBERS - wrong time, open door, covered eye, empty house, goodnight."

That is the pole order, matching what changed in the house:

1. **CLOCK** (the impossible time)
2. **DOOR** (the ajar bedroom door)
3. **EYE** (the covered mirror)
4. **HOUSE** (the empty house photo)
5. **MOON** (the goodnight message)

The TREE pole is never correct. A wrong pole resets all of them. When the distant house lights up, a DOOR appears between two poles - go through it.

## Day 3 - The Power Came Back

Tasks: look at the TV static, listen to the radio, listen to the buzzing wall (kitchen junction box), and check the fuse room - the basement door (east side of the kitchen) is finally unlocked; the fuse box is inside. Then sleep.

### Dream 3: The Hospital

Strict order, all at or near the nurse station alcove (east side of the corridor):

1. Read the **PATIENT WRISTBAND** on the desk ("J. DOE. ROOM - WHERE THEY SLEEP.").
2. Take **CHART 204** ("DOES NOT WAKE") - the other charts are flavor only, no penalty.
3. Open the **MEDICINE CABINET** on the alcove wall (unlocks after the chart). Take the bottle.
4. Go to **ROOM 204** (the room with your bed, last door) and use the **HEART MONITOR**.

The ward lights up and a discharge paper appears near the exit. Leave through the **WARD EXIT** at the far end.

## Day 4 - What Followed You Back

Tasks: read the medical document on the kitchen table, play the clinic answering machine message, open the bathroom cabinet (medication), open the bedroom dresser drawer (bracelet + photo). Then sleep.

### Dream 4: The Gas Station

Read the **RECEIPT CLUE** on the counter: "1 THING TO REMEMBER. 1 THING TO WATCH. 1 THING TO OPEN."

Collect the three memories:

- **OLD PHOTO** (remember) - middle aisle, deep end
- **VHS TAPE** (watch) - right aisle, deep end
- **HOUSE KEY** (open) - hook on the back wall, labeled YOURS

**For the secret ending later, ALSO grab the AA BATTERIES (right aisle, front) and the ROAD MAP (middle aisle, front) now.** Soup and the milk carton are decoys but harmless.

Check out at the **OLD REGISTER**. A door labeled **HOME** appears on the road outside - go through it.

## Day 5 - The Things You Bought

Required tasks: read the receipt on the kitchen counter, play the VHS in the VCR (under the TV), open the kitchen drawer (dream key fits now), and try the front door once.

> **SECRET ENDING - only available today. See below before sleeping.**

Then sleep. You wake up in the same house. That is the point.

## Day 6 - The House After Sleep

Every dream is leaking in. Put five objects back:

1. **Hallway clock** - on the living room couch area. Place it.
2. **Field fuse** - on the fuse box in the basement fuse room. Seat it.
3. **Chart 204** - on your bed pillow. File it.
4. **Receipt** - on the kitchen table. Smooth it out.
5. **VHS tape** - on the VCR. Play it.

When the fifth is placed: "Somewhere inside the front door, something stops being locked."

## The Four Endings

All choices happen after the door unlocks on Day 6 (except the secret).

### Ending 1 - Morning (Leave the House)

Open the front door. Walk outside onto the porch. Done.

### Ending 2 - Routine (Stay Inside)

Open the front door. Then interact with it again to close it. Done.

### Ending 3 - Dream House (Go Back to Sleep)

Ignore the open door. Go back to the bed and sleep. Done.

### Ending 4 - Secret (The Front Door, Early)

Must be done on **Day 5**, before sleeping:

1. In the gas station dream (Day 4 night), buy the **AA batteries** and the **road map** along with the three memories.
2. On Day 5, use the **TV remote** on the couch (only exists if you bought batteries).
3. Study the **impossible map** on the living room wall (only exists if you bought the map).
4. Try the front door once (this is also a required task - it opens an inch and slams).
5. Try the front door a **second time**. It opens all the way onto endless wheat.
