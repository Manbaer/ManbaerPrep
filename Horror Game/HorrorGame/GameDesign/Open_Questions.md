## Story Questions

- What specific event caused the player to stop leaving the house?
- Who used to live in the house?
- Is the player remembering a real person, avoiding guilt, or being preserved by the house?
- Is the house actively conscious, or is it a reflection of the player's routine and memory?
- What is the exact meaning of the hospital dream?
- Should the canon ending imply the player escaped, was copied, or was always a dream version?

## Dream Questions

- How many dream environments should be in the full game?
- Are dreams always entered by sleeping in bed, or can some dreams invade the house while awake?
- Should wrong puzzle interactions only reset the dream, or also worsen the real house?
- Should the dream order be fixed or partially player-driven?
- Should the wheat field puzzle rely on visual symbols, audio humming, or both?
- Should the gas station dream require exactly three purchased items every time?

## House Questions

- What rooms exist in the final house layout?
- Which rooms are locked at the start?
- Which dream unlocks the basement, garage, utility room, or fuse room?
- How much should the house visibly decay over time?
- Can the player ever look outside through normal windows?
- Should the front door be interactable from the beginning?

## Progression Questions

- What are the required story flags for each dream?
- Which flags are optional and only affect ending variations?
- Can players miss important story clues?
- Should the answering machine messages play in fixed order or unlock by dream flags?
- How should VHS tapes be discovered and played?

## Ending Questions

- Is Leave the House the canon ending?
- How early can the secret front door ending be unlocked?
- What hidden conditions trigger the secret ending?
- Should the final choice be completely silent and movement-based?
- What final image should be used for the canon ending?

## Prototype Questions

- What exact object order should the first hallway puzzle use?
- What should the first answering machine message say after waking?
- What photo changes in the bedroom?
- What is inside the newly unlocked closet?
- What should the player learn from the first complete dream-to-house loop?
