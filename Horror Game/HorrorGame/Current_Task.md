  
Build the first simple prototype in Unity.  
  
The prototype should include:  
  
1. A first-person player controller.  
2. A simple 1990s-style house room.  
3. Three simple tasks:  
- Check the TV.  
- Check the answering machine.  
- Turn off the kitchen light.  
4. A bed the player can interact with.  
5. When the player uses the bed, switch to a dream hallway scene.  
6. In the dream hallway, the player can interact with one strange door.  
7. After interacting with the door, return to the house.  
8. When the player wakes up, something in the house should be different.  
  
Keep everything simple and beginner-friendly.  
Use clear C# scripts.  
Add comments explaining what the scripts do.
