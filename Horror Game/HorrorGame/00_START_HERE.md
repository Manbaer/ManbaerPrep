# Dream House Horror Game

This is a Unity psychological horror game about a shut-in living alone in a 1990s house.

During the day, the player repeats a quiet routine inside the house. At night, they enter surreal dream spaces. What they fix, break, find, or choose in the dreams rewrites the real house.

## Main Loop

Dream environment
-> dream puzzle
-> symbolic meaning
-> real-world consequence
-> new house change or story flag

The strongest mechanic is:

> What you fix, break, find, or choose in the dream rewrites the real house.

## First Prototype Target

Build one complete dream-to-reality loop before expanding the rest of the game.

Recommended first loop:

1. Player wakes up in the bedroom.
2. Player completes simple house tasks.
3. Player goes to bed.
4. Player enters the endless bedroom hallway dream.
5. Player solves the object-memory puzzle.
6. Player reaches the bedroom door.
7. Player wakes up.
8. The bedroom photo has changed.
9. The answering machine has a new message.
10. The closet is now unlocked.

See [[Prototype_Vertical_Slice]] for the first playable slice.

## Tone

- Lonely
- Slow
- Surreal
- Unsettling
- Ambiguous
- 1990s domestic horror

The game is very WIP. It should avoid explaining everything directly. The player should piece the story together through changed rooms, photos, answering machine messages, VHS tapes, receipts, medical papers, and environmental details.
