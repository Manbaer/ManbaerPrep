Dream House Horror Game  
  
You are a person living alone in the 1990s.  (hikikomori)
  
During the day, you live a quiet routine inside your house:  
- Wake up  
- Eat  
- Watch TV  
- Check answering machine  
- Do chores  
- Go to bed  
  
When you sleep, you enter strange dream places:  
- Endless roads  
- Endless hallways  
- Endless forests  
- Empty malls  
- Repeating houses  
  
Actions in the dream affect the real world.  
  
Example:  
If the player opens a strange door in the dream, a new room appears in the real house.  
  
The game is psychological horror. It should feel lonely, surreal, slow, and unsettling
